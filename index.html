Our Story

CR.K9 Villa

Every training journey runs in structured 3-month phases — building skill, confidence, and trust at every stage.

What We Offer

Our Training Programs

All programs run in 3-month phases. Each new level adds 3 more months of advanced training.

Obedience Training

Foundation-level training covering basic commands, leash manners, impulse control, and social behaviour. Perfect for new dog owners.

Phase 1:3 months — Core Commands
Phase 2:6 months — Advanced Obedience
Phase 3:9 months — Off-Leash Mastery

Guard Training

Develop your dog's natural protective instincts into a reliable, controlled guarding ability — ideal for properties and businesses.

Phase 1:3 months — Alert & Bark Training
Phase 2:6 months — Perimeter Security
Phase 3:9 months — Advanced Guard Work

Personal Protection

Elite one-on-one protection training — your dog learns to detect threats and respond with precision under command.

Phase 1:3 months — Foundation Bite Work
Phase 2:6 months — Scenario Training
Phase 3:9 months — Real-World Protection

Family Protection

Tailored for households — your dog becomes a loyal, safe, and responsive protector of every family member, including children.

Phase 1:3 months — Family Integration
Phase 2:6 months — Protective Instincts
Phase 3:9 months — Full Family Guard
